Thursday Muffins Bakery

Fresh vegan muffins made with care.

Thursday Muffins is a small community-focused vegan home bakery run by Welensa. We make delicious muffins every Thursday for pickup and local events.

Details

Our recipes are refined over time — quality matters more than speed. We sometimes borrow techniques from classic pastry-making, like a flaky technique inspired by pâtisserie craft.
Because we bake in small batches, flavors stay rich and fresh.

Welensa started baking as a hobby and now balances baking with nursing studies. Orders are accepted in advance for Thursday pickup.

Caption: Our friendly muffin logo — come taste the joy!

Fun Facts

We use seasonal, plant-based ingredients whenever possible.

This is very special: every Thursday we test one new flavor for the community.

Welensa moved to the US three years ago and started baking here as a way to connect with neighbors.
